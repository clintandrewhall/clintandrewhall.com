About This List

Introducing Our Second-hand Registry List

There are so many things we need to welcome our daughter to the world, but there are so many things that need not be new...! This is a list of items we would prefer be gifted to us second-hand... well-loved or never used, from your basement or from a thrift store.

Rest assured, we'll be passing the love on to another child, (either ours or someone else's). ❤️

Things to Read & Play

Books

Children's Books

We both adore books, and they're sure our daughter will love them, too! It would be awesome for people to find or bring any second-hand books that they think she'll adore. Books that give Clint a chance to use funny voices are an obvious added bonus...!

Things to Wear

Clothing

Gender-neutral Clothing

We're having a girl, but we're keeping it neutral! Please send us your favorite outfits, clothes you wish you'd found time to try, or anything you see that you think would be fabulous.

Based on many, many recommendations, here's a list of items... but don't feel constrained! Anything and everything will be passed on!

  • Undershirts or onesies (mix of short-sleeve and long-sleeve)
  • Nightgowns
  • One-piece stretchy sleepers (any with zippers?)
  • Pairs of pants
  • Newborn hats
  • Socks or booties, to wear with nightgowns and outfits
  • Scratch mittens, to keep baby from scratching his face
  • Cardigans or jackets
  • Outfits for dressing up
